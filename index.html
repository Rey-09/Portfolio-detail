About Me

Since middle school, I have been passionate about art and creativity, starting with drawing, then exploring animation, fashion design, and eventually web design. I enjoy turning ideas into digital works that are not only visually appealing but also functional.

For my personally speaking, a creation should ideally not only carry aesthetic value but also provide benefits for others. With a background as a designer and developer, I strive to create digital products that are easy to use, comfortable, and engaging.

Outside of that, I enjoy drawing both traditionally and digitally, and I often look for creative solutions to everyday problems that later inspire my UI/UX work.
